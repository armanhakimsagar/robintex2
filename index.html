OUR MANUFACTURING CAPABILITIES

Dyeing & Finishing

At Robintex, sustainability meets performance. Our eco-friendly dyeing plants handle 24 tons per day across both tubular and open-width formats. Complemented by an in-house finishing capacity of 25 tons per day, we use top-tier equipment from Monforts, LK, and Alkan to guarantee world-class quality, faster turnaround times, and unmatched reliability for our global partners.

Crafting Fabrics That Move the World

Robintex redefines fabric production through advanced computerized knitting technologies. From soft Single Jersey to robust Double Jersey, dynamic Rib structures, performance-driven Elastane blends, elegant Piqué, sustainable Bamboo, and cozy Terry Fleece — we create fabrics to inspire. Our versatile yarn blending expertise covers Cotton, Cotton-Polyester, Modal, Viscose, Poly-Viscose, Tencel, Coolmax®, and beyond, supporting the next generation of global fashion and activewear brands.

Printing: Bringing Colors and Textures to Life

At Robintex, we combine German engineering with creative expertise to deliver cutting-edge printing solutions. Our Hebbecker automatic printers masterfully handle up to 12 colors and produce 60,000 pieces daily. From vibrant pigments to intricate heat transfers, high-density textures to striking embellishments — we bring designs to life with unmatched quality and precision.

Cutting: Where Accuracy Shapes Excellence

At Robintex, every cut matters. Our cutting technology ensures flawless accuracy, consistent replication, and reduced material waste — setting a benchmark for quality craftsmanship and production efficiency.

Laboratory: Precision Testing for Quality and Safety

At Robintex, our state-of-the-art laboratory is built to test every aspect of fabric performance. From color fastness and shrinkage to pilling resistance and eco-sustainability, we ensure that our products meet the highest industry standards. With technologies from the EU and US, we also assess the environmental impact and human health safety, making sure that every piece aligns with our commitment to responsible manufacturing.

ETP & POWER

Robintex’s 120-cubic-meter-per-hour Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP) is equipped with advanced technologies that efficiently dehydrate sludge and treat dyeing effluents through both chemical and biological processes. This ensures the highest standards of environmental sustainability. We source all our water from deep-water pumps, which is then rigorously purified through a state-of-the-art, US-made treatment plant, reinforcing our commitment to clean, responsible manufacturing practices. Our 2 MW gas and diesel operated captive power backup system ensures uninterrupted electricity supply round the clock.

ETHICAL AND SUSTAINABLE WORK PRACTICES

Find The Fabric, Enjoy The Process and The Results

The organization is strongly committed to worker welfare, sustainability, and ethical operations. It ensures fair wages above the minimum level, safe working environments with regular fire drills, functional fire safety systems, nutritious meals, clean drinking water, on-site healthcare facilities, maternity support, and childcare centers for workers’ children. In terms of environmental sustainability, the company operates Effluent Treatment Plants (ETPs) for responsible wastewater management, uses biomass fuel-based power generation up to 1,200 KW, and runs 3.2 MW of solar power. It is a PLATINUM LEED-certified green factory, incorporating solar energy, rainwater harvesting, energy-efficient lighting, and recycling programs to reduce plastic and textile waste. The company actively engages with the local community by ensuring access to safe drinking water and sanitation, offering scholarships for workers’ children, and organizing eye camps, blood donation drives, tree plantation programs, and health awareness campaigns. It maintains transparency and ethical sourcing through regular social audits (BSCI, WRAP, Sedex), ethical supplier vetting, and participation in initiatives like Accord/RSC and Better Work Bangladesh. Worker participation is encouraged through formal committees, welfare teams, anonymous grievance systems, and continuous training and professional development programs.
